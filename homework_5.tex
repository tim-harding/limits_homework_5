\subsection*{2}
Testing the convergence of $\sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{1}{n (\ln(n))^2}$

\begin{align*}
    u &= \ln(x) \\
    du &= \frac{dx}{x} \\
    dx &= x\ du
\end{align*}
\begin{align*}
     & \sum_{\ln(2)}^\infty u^{-2}\ du \\
    =& \hard{-\frac{1}{u}}_{\ln(2)}^\infty \\
    =& -\frac{1}{\infty} - \round{-\frac{1}{\ln(2)}} \\
    =& \frac{1}{\ln(2)}
\end{align*}

The series converges.
